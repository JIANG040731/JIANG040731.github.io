My Story: A Journey to Sanxingdui

Hello! My name is JIANG HONGYU, and I’m currently a third-year university student. During the summer, I had the chance to visit the Sanxingdui Museum in Chengdu, China. It was one of the most unforgettable cultural experiences of my life.

As I stood in front of the ancient bronze trees and mysterious masks, I felt a deep connection to history. These artifacts, created thousands of years ago, were both majestic and strange—almost alien. I couldn’t believe how sophisticated the craftsmanship was. It made me think about how many stories are buried in time, waiting to be rediscovered.

Visiting Sanxingdui reminded me that learning doesn’t only happen in classrooms or online—it happens when we explore the world around us. This experience taught me to stay curious, keep asking questions, and appreciate the past. That’s something I try to bring into everything I study.
